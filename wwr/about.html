History

Juice Rafting was founded with a passion for adventure and love for the wild waters. What started as a small group of enthusiasts is now a fully established guide company known for its safety, fun, and unforgettable trips.

Our company has grown to become one of the most trusted names in rafting, thanks to our incredible guides, amazing customers, and breathtaking locations.
